## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Witt is the AI-powered study companion built for every student, everywhere.

Whether you're preparing for the SAT, GRE, WAEC, JAMB, IELTS, or any of 100+ standardized exams — Witt adapts to your learning style, tracks your progress, and helps you study smarter with AI.

**Sage AI Tutor**
Chat with your personal AI tutor 24/7. Get step-by-step homework help, instant explanations, and personalized study plans. Free users get Groq AI; Premium users get GPT-4o.

**Test Prep Engine**
Adaptive practice questions for 100+ exams. AI generates new questions based on your weak areas. Pre-generated question banks available offline.

**Smart Flashcards**
5 study modes including spaced repetition (SM-2). Create decks, import CSV, or let AI generate them from your notes.

**Mock Tests**
Full-length timed simulations with real exam conditions. Detailed score analytics and topic breakdowns.

**Educational Games**
9 games including Word Duel, Quiz Royale, and Spelling Bee. Daily brain challenges and multiplayer (Premium).

**Study Community**
Join study groups, ask questions in the forum, share flashcard decks, and challenge friends.

**Works Offline**
Full offline support. Download content packs for airplane mode studying. Available in 21 languages.

Free: 3 games/day · 1 post/day · 10 AI messages/day
Premium: Unlimited + GPT-4o + multiplayer
