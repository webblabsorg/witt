## Description

Witt is the AI-powered study companion built for every student, everywhere.

Whether you're preparing for the SAT, GRE, WAEC, JAMB, IELTS, or any of 100+ standardized exams — Witt adapts to your learning style, tracks your progress, and helps you study smarter with AI.

**Sage AI Tutor**
Chat with your personal AI tutor 24/7. Get step-by-step homework help, instant explanations, and personalized study plans.

**Test Prep Engine**
Adaptive practice questions for 100+ exams with AI-generated content.

**Smart Flashcards**
5 study modes including spaced repetition. Create, import, or AI-generate decks.

**Mock Tests**
Full-length timed simulations with real exam conditions and score analytics.

**Educational Games**
9 games including Word Duel, Quiz Royale, and Spelling Bee.

**Study Community**
Study groups, Q&A forum, deck marketplace, and friend challenges.

**Full Offline Support**
Download content packs. Works in airplane mode. 21 languages.

**Windows-specific features:**
- Native title bar and window resizing
- Keyboard-first navigation
- Windows Toast notifications via OneSignal
- Microsoft Store IAP for subscriptions
